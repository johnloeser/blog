About me

I am a PhD Candidate in Agricultural and Resource Economics at UC Berkeley. I am on the 2018-2019 academic job market.

I am a development economist, and my research focuses on agricultural productivity in developing countries. My job market paper measures the welfare effects of access to groundwater for agriculture in India and implications for optimal policy. Ongoing work concerns the roles of labor and land markets in adoption of irrigation for off-season vegetable cultivation in Rwanda.
